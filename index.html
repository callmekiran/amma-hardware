About Us

For over 75 years, Amma Hardware has been a cornerstone of the community. Established in 1950, we began with a simple mission: to provide our neighbors with the tools they need to build their dreams. Today, that mission remains unchanged. We understand that quality shouldn't come at a premium. That’s why we’ve spent decades perfecting the balance between high-end materials and the market's most competitive prices. From the foundation of your home to the tools in your garden, Amma Hardware is where quality meets affordability..
